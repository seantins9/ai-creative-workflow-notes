AI Creative Workflow Notes

Three lightweight AI tools for checking visual creative direction before publishing social, product, or campaign assets.

Image to video

ClipTrend.ai helps turn a still image or text prompt into a short AI video for social-ready visual tests.

Outfit previews

AIClothSwap is useful for previewing outfit and clothing changes on a person image before a shoot or campaign asset.

Hairstyle previews

AIChangeHair lets you test hairstyle and haircut ideas from a portrait while keeping the face, clothing, and lighting natural.

The practical workflow is to test one visual element at a time, compare the result with the original, and then decide whether it belongs in the final creative.
